Who We Are

Republic International Residential School and College is a premier educational institution located in Mangalam, near Hanur, in the Chamarajanagar district of Karnataka. Established with a strong commitment to academic excellence and character building, the institution offers the ICSE curriculum in a well-structured residential environment.

Our school provides education from early childhood through higher secondary levels, ensuring a seamless and nurturing academic journey for every student. We believe that education is most effective when it balances intellectual growth with emotional, physical, and social development.

With experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and a student-centric approach, Republic International Residential School and College creates a disciplined yet joyful learning atmosphere where students are encouraged to explore their talents, develop confidence, and achieve their highest potential.

As a residential institution, we provide a structured and caring campus life that helps students develop independence, responsibility, and strong interpersonal skills. Our hostels are designed to be safe, comfortable, and supportive, with dedicated wardens and staff who ensure students feel at home while maintaining discipline and routine.

Our Vision & Mission

At Republic International Residential School and College, our mission is to provide a well-rounded education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, discipline, and ethical values. We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring residential environment where students feel supported and motivated to learn.

Our vision is to shape responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Through the ICSE curriculum, smart classrooms, well-equipped science and mathematics laboratories, and interactive teaching methods, we empower students with critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

Beyond academics, we place strong emphasis on arts, sports, physical education, and life skills. Music, dance, craft, and sports activities are integrated into daily school life, ensuring holistic development and fostering a lifelong love for learning.
